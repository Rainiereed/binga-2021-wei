Let's Go!

At Binga Beach we go beyond regular hotels and resorts by providing an extraordinary array of adventure activities. As much as we love lounging on the beach, we’re here to push our own boundaries, to really get out to the horizon and explore.

We include a hearty handful of equipment usage free of charge with your room stay; you’re free to use the native fishing boat kayaks, stand-up-paddleboards, mountain bikes and more all you like.

Our other tours are available to book alongside booking your cabin, here online later if you like, or simply during your stay with us. It’s best to book ahead, though, to ensure a slot to do what you like.

Kayaking

These aren’t the regular old plastic tourist kayaks you’re used to; these are 100% native smallcraft fisherman outriggers. Tons of stability and easy to handle, but we’re there to help you launch at the beach all you like.

First come first serve, though reservations can be made, check with beach crew.


Stand-Up-Paddleboarding

Ready for action! From Binga Beach there are amazing beaches around the corner just hundreds of meters away to adventure to, and can go farther if you have the strength for it! The SUPs are also great for just playing around right at the shore.

These are subject to availability, because if our boat goes out for island hopping they will probably take them along. Otherwise they are available at the beachfront, first come first serve.


Beach Lounging

Does it count as a sport? We’re going to go with a yes. Because at Binga it includes swimming, looking for seashells, going for long walks up and down the beach you have all to yourself, enjoying quality lounge chairs and shade, maybe even some racquetball or volleyball, soccer? Cocktails? Yes.

Island Hopping Tour

Hit the high seas on our own fast and sturdy boat the ZeaStar as we take you to the best secret and uninhabited islands and beaches. In our part of Palawan there are no crowds, and we go to gorgeous beaches where you’ll have the place all to yourself for as long as you like.

Those on the tour choose where they want to go, whether to one beach, multiple beaches, beaches + snorkeling spots etc. The choice is yours.

Minimum 3 persons required to book the boat and the maximum capacity is 10 persons. For larger groups simply call ahead and we’ll schedule our buddy’s boats to join us and get the whole flotilla going!

Also included is snorkeling at the beaches or stopping at the best local snorkeling spots. Stand-up-paddleboards can also brought along for guest’s use. Trip flexible but usually goes from 9 AM to 4 PM.

$37 / 1850PHP per guest

Minimum 3 guests (or equal booking) to schedule boat

Snorkeling

For most folks we recommend the Island Hopping Tour above, as you enjoy the comfort of the big boat, get lunch included, and get a lot of remote gorgeous relaxing beach time included.

For the hardy adventurer, however, we launch out on a smaller fishing boat and go right to the best sites for dedicated snorkeling and free diving swims.

No lunch included but you’re welcome to bring snacks and we can pack a lunch for you from our menu if you like. The trip is shorter at just a few hours, your choice morning or afternoon.

Snorkeling gear provided.

$20 / 1000PHP per guest

No guest minimum, howerver 1 day advanced notice required. Subject to boat availability and favorable sea conditions.
